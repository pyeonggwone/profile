Capacity Constraints in Asia East
Due to sustained high demand, Microsoft has implemented capacity preservation measures in 
the Asia East Azure region. These measures prioritize existing customers and include:
• DC Constraints: We are currently navigating DC constraints in AZ03 impacting Intel and 
AMD capacity. 
• Disabled auto-approval for quota increases and manual triage of quota requests in 
partnership with sales leadership team.
• For regional deployments, customers can deploy regionally for Intel and AMD
• For zonal deployments, we will be able to support the demands in two zones (AZ01 and 
AZ02)
• These constraints impact Azure IaaS and PaaS offerings. PaaS products that offer zonal 
redundancy (replication across all 3 AZs) are more heavily impacted.
Key Dates:
• Asia East AZ03 has long term constraints for Intel and AMD, resolution timelines is 
H1FY27
Recommended Action:
To ensure deployment success and scalability, customers are encouraged to:
• Consider leveraging two zonal or multi-region or regional deployments. A multi-region 
approach achieves high availability, ensures resiliency and enables advanced 
scalability.
• Customer is advised to consider leveraging other Asia DCs - Malaysia West or Indonesia 
Central.
• Microsoft is actively monitoring capacity and working cross-functionally to support 
customer growth.
Additional Communication
Customers can also consider alternate region for their zonal deployments till August 2026 in
Indonesia Central or Malaysia West. Till then, we can support the two zone deployments. If 
none of these alternates work for the customer, please follow the escalation process.
Microsoft continues to closely monitor the situation and work to increase available capacity in 
the Asia East region in line with customer demand signals.
In the meantime, several Microsoft teams are working around the clock to identify solutions to 
help meet your additional capacity requests to ensure continued growth. Representatives from 
our product team and DC Leads from each time zone join frequent status calls with our teams 
to ensure clear transparency and prioritization until these preventative restrictions are lifted.
We will work with you on a regular update cadence, and share more information about your 
specific quota requests, and the ongoing efforts to expand our cloud infrastructure in the 
region. We apologize for the inconvenience and extra work that these preventative 
measures represent and stand ready to assist you in successfully running and growing your 
business in our Asia East datacentres and beyond.
