## Project Description, Background, Scope and Objectives

A corporate operations team (~30 people) is moving from occasional, self-directed use of Microsoft 365 Copilot to a structured, team-wide adoption where Copilot becomes the default tool for common daily work tasks.

Copilot licenses are already available to all team members, but current usage is entirely individual and inconsistent. Some employees use it occasionally out of curiosity; most have tried it once or twice and returned to their prior habits. The change is to shift from optional and personal to structured and expected: the team establishes a shared standard for using Copilot in email drafting, meeting summarization, status reporting, and document preparation.

This is not a technology rollout. The technology exists and is accessible. The change is behavioral — building new work habits across a diverse team of people with varying digital fluency, competing priorities, and different relationships with AI tools.

**Background:**
- Productivity pressure: The team is expected to deliver more with the same headcount. Copilot directly reduces time spent on low-value writing and summarizing tasks that currently consume 1–2 hours per person per day.
- Wasted license investment: Copilot licenses have been purchased and deployed. Without structured adoption, usage remains below 20% and the investment is not realized.
- Future readiness: Organizations that embed AI habits now build durable advantages.

**Scope:**
- Scale: ~30 people, one internal team
- Timeline: 3 months
- Tool: Microsoft 365 Copilot (already licensed and deployed)
- Process changes: Email drafting, Teams meeting summaries, weekly status updates, internal document preparation
- Complexity: Low-to-moderate. No budget required, no system change, no compliance barriers. Core challenge is habit formation under active workload.

**Objectives:**
- Within 3 months, every team member regularly uses Copilot for at least 3 recurring work tasks
- Build a shared prompt library collaboratively by team leads and early adopters
- Realize return on Copilot license investment
