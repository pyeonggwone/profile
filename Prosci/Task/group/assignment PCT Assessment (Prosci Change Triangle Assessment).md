## PCT Summary

| Element | Score | Interpretation |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| Sponsorship / Leadership | 23 / 30 | Alert / possible risk |
| Project Management | 20 / 30 | Alert / possible risk |

**Overall PCT conclusion:** The project has a strong strategic rationale and visible sponsors, but it is not yet equally strong across all three sides of the Prosci Change Triangle.
